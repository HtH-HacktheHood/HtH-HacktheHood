<!-- About The Company -->
#### Hack the hood provides youth and communities of color with tech skill-building programs and career navigation support that are grounded in justice and ensure economic mobility. We affirm that ‚Äúeducation is the practice of freedom‚Äù and is achieved through dialogue and the awakening of critical consciousness. At Hack the Hood our goal is to create a community of care that supports early-career Black, Latinx, AAPI, and Indigenous talent in their technology career journeys. One of the most powerful ways we do this is by matching our young people with mentors they can identify with and who can be a champion, coach, and trusted industry connection as they pursue their career journeys.
